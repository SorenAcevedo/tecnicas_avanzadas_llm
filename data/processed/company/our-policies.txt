Our Policies
We are working hard to reimagine a healthier future for all people, their pets and our planet. To reinforce and guide the way we fulfill this purpose around the world, we’ve designed a set of policies to clearly communicate our dedication to doing the right thing -from the way we work to the people we serve, from the performance of our products to our respect for the environment.
