Message About Commitment to Good Corporate Governance from Noel Wallace
Governance is an ongoing commitment shared by our Board of Directors, our management and all Colgate people. The Board believes that good corporate governance accompanies and greatly aids our long-term business success and that we have consistently been at the forefront of good corporate governance. Reflecting its commitment to continuous improvement, the Board reviews its governance practices on an ongoing basis to ensure that they promote stakeholder value. We invite you to learn more about our corporate governance policies and practices and our Board of Directors by exploring this website.
